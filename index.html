Our Mission: Bridging the Gap Between Waste and Want

In Ahmedabad, thousands of people struggle with food insecurity while tonnes of perfectly good food goes to waste every day. The Community Fridge Initiative was born from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could connect the two?

We believe that access to nutritious food is a basic human right, and it should never come with stigma or shame. Our community fridges provide a dignified, accessible solution where anyone can take what they need, and local businesses can redirect their surplus food to those who need it most.
